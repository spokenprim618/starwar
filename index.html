Star Wars

What is Star Wars

Star wars is a large universe with a deep history of 25,000 years showing the different eras of good and evil through the force. It was directed and created with the first movie being episode 4 by George Lucas in 1977. The movies focus on the time close to the fall of the jedi, the rise of the empire, and the rebellion. The main George Lucas films focus on the skywalker's for example Anakin Skywalker and Luke skywalker. While after this main line disney took over the franchise. There is more material such as books and comics that further the characters stories and detail the large history of the universe.

Creator

George Walton Lucas Jr. from California, born in 1944 and became a film director/producer in 1965. He not only created star wars but Indiana Jones and founded many movie studies such as Lucasfilm.
